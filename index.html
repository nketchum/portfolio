Lettering Reveals Essence

Walt Disney once said the “primary purpose of any fine art is to elicit a purely emotional reaction in the beholder.” Whether the act of drawing beautiful letters—known as “lettering”—should be considered art is irrelevant. Hand-drawn lettering holds a deeper dimension to the written word, and reveals the underlying spirit of the author’s message.
