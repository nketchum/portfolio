About This Site

This website was designed and produced by Nicholas Ketchum for a class project at the University of Michigan School of Information in Ann Arbor, Michigan. It's partly a work of love and partly a work of academic grind. There's a balance.

Handwritten lettering is a very interesting field of study that helps shed light on the spirit and value behind what's written. Form combines with function; professional lettering must be legible, long lasting, and ideally quickly written.

This website shows only a small slice of the various European traditions. Some styles are missing entirely, while others are underrepresented. I may maintain this site in the future as a personal reference for my personal lettering hobby.

Nicholas Ketchum

UMSI Student & Digital Consultant
